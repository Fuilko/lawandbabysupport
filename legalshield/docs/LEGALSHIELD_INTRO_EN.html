1. Self-Introduction — What is LegalShield?

LegalShield is a FREE AI-powered support system for crime victims and rights violation victims.

In Japan, over 2 million people fall victim to crimes or rights violations annually, but 70% say "I don't know who to talk to," "I don't have time to save evidence," or "I'm afraid the police will refuse to file my report"— they suffer alone. LegalShield was born to break that invisible wall.

Core Mission
Victims do not need to become legal experts.
AI organizes the situation, preserves evidence, analyzes law, simulates strategy, and refers experts —
so every victim can take the right next step within the "golden hours" after an incident.
🛡️
24/7 · Free · Anonymous
No real name or address required. Open your phone and use it instantly.
🤖
AI 5-Role System
Emergency response, evidence preservation, legal analysis, strategy simulation, and expert referral — all in one.
🔥
Anti-Grafting Feature
AI auto-generates legal-basis reports and police negotiation scripts to prevent case dismissal.
📊
Japan's Largest Legal Database
623K national laws, 724K precedents, 885 e-Stat tables — instant RAG search.

2. AI 5-Role System

LegalShield's AI simultaneously performs 5 expert roles, supporting victims from incident occurrence through expert matching.

RoleFunctionHelp for Victims
🚨 Emergency Responder One-touch dial 110 / #8898 / #7119, GPS sharing, silent mode, 10-sec countdown auto-alert In life-threatening moments, just press — no thinking required
📁 Evidence Collector Auto-organize photos, recordings, documents. SHA-256 hash, timestamp, GPS tag, AES-256 encryption, auto preservation certificate Evidence never lost, never tampered, court-admissible
⚖️ Legal Analyst RAG search 623K laws + 724K precedents, instant relevant article suggestion, auto crime classification "What is my situation called in law?"
🧭 Strategy Simulator Criminal complaint, civil suit, ADR mediation, administrative appeal, protection order — compare 5 paths by time, cost, success rate "Which path is best for my current situation?"
🗺️ Expert Referral Navigator GPS-linked matching to nearest support (bar associations, NPOs, legal aid, shelters), booking scripts, document checklist, fee waiver verification "Who near me can help?"

🛡️ Unique Feature: Anti-Grafting (Prevent Case Dismissal)
Before the victim goes to the police station, AI auto-generates a "Legal Basis Report" and "Police Negotiation Script":
• Police: "This is civil." → You counter with the applicable Penal Code article.
• Police: "Insufficient evidence." → You explain that filing a report does NOT require complete evidence.
• Police: "We'll record it as a consultation." → You demand a formal acceptance number.
If still refused, AI auto-generates complaints to the Prefectural Public Safety Commission and the Prosecution Review Board.

3. Developer Profile — A ¥68M Lesson That Became a Weapon for the Weak

This document is not just a project proposal.
It is a declaration of uprising from someone who has faced the wall of justice alone.

Who am I? I am Kenji (Liu Chien-Chih) — a Master's student at Kochi University Forestry Engineering Lab, CEO of Hi-i Forest Co., Ltd., and a survivor of a ¥68 million corporate fraud.
I studied criminal neuroscience, criminal/civil/environmental law, and once dreamed of becoming a prosecutor. I have managed factory safety at Apple and NIKE, and hold a technical partnership with Taiwan's TEAMS 5 (cybersecurity).
I am not a stranger to law, technology, or business. Yet even I was powerless.

⚠️ The Catalyst: A ¥68 Million Corporate Fraud
On February 3, 2026, a drone I purchased for forestry surveying crashed in Taiwan due to a design defect.
Investigation revealed the manufacturer had concealed 34 safety defects — including disabled battery protections, a magnetometer 5x over safety limits, and a "0.0.0" development firmware shipped as production-ready.
Administrative agencies and consumer protection bodies were powerless to act. The manufacturer threatened supply cutoff and dismissed all responsibility.
I searched over a dozen law firms across Kochi, Osaka, and Tokyo. None could help at a price I could afford.
Currently litigating simultaneously in Taiwan and Japan (breach of trust / property damage).

This ¥68 million disaster was the catalyst.
I personally disassembled the drone's firmware line by line, analyzed Japanese civil and administrative law, and built a forensic evidence database that grew to thousands of pages.
Without this nightmare, I would never have understood how desperate ordinary people feel when they stand before the judicial wall with no money, no knowledge, and no ally.

💡 The Equivalent Exchange
A forestry project was destroyed. But from its ashes rose something far more powerful:
LegalShield — a system to fight the "locust effect" of corporate fraud, to empower those with no voice, and to ensure no one faces injustice alone.
One industry project lost. A social safety net born.

I raise 4 daughters in Kochi Prefecture — the land of the Kuroshio Current, far from Tokyo's legal resources.
I feel the overwhelming resource gap between urban and rural areas every single day. Regional revitalization is not just economics. Law, healthcare, and justice are distant dreams for rural families.
Especially for women, children, and the elderly, proper legal support is orders of magnitude harder to access than in cities.

This is why LegalShield exists:

"When public power fails to protect ordinary people,
technology must become a weapon in the hands of the weak."
— Kenji (Liu Chien-Chih), May 2026
4 Core Observations
📉
Observation 1: System Gaps
Japan has 2M+ victims annually, but Legal Aid is weekdays 10-16 only, NPOs have regional gaps, and police case dismissal is frequent. Victims receive almost no structured support within the 24-72 hour "golden time."
💻
Observation 2: Tech Inequality
Enterprise Legal Tech (e.g., Legalscape) is mature, but targets "companies that can hire lawyers." Ordinary citizens, victims, and the vulnerable are the ones left behind by technology.
🧠
Observation 3: AI Potential
In 2025-2026, AI (LLM) became capable of high-quality legal document analysis, strategy simulation, and multilingual dialogue. This capability should not belong only to large corporations — it must be open to everyone who needs help.
⚖️
Observation 4: Urban-Rural Gap
In rural areas like Kochi Prefecture, access to lawyers, NPOs, and support agencies is extremely limited. For female victims, parents, and the elderly, resources in Tokyo and Osaka are distant, with overwhelming travel costs and time constraints. Delivering the power of law to rural areas digitally is an urgent priority.

5.1 How to Embed the Database into an APP?

Step 1: Choose an APP Framework

FrameworkProsConsRating
FlutterSingle codebase for iOS + Android, high performance, flexible UIRequires learning Dart⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
React NativeJavaScript ecosystem, many developers, rich librariesSlightly lower performance, version fragmentation⭐⭐⭐⭐
PWA (Web APP)Fastest release, no review, web-to-app instantlyNative feature limitations⭐⭐⭐⭐
Best for MVP

Step 2: On-Device AI Inference (Offline Capable)

The victim's phone may be in a basement, airplane mode, or unstable signal area.
ONNX Runtime Mobile or TensorFlow Lite to convert AI models for on-device execution conversion.
5-role inference completes entirely on-device; recordings, photos, and location data are NEVER sent to servers.

Step 3: Device-Embedded Vector Database

LanceDB supports embedded/serverless mode.
623K law vectors compressed to ~500MB on-device database.
Sync at first download, then incremental updates only (~10-50MB/week).
Legal search completes on-device with zero network latency.

Step 4: On-Device Encryption of Sensitive Data

Evidence photos, recordings, dialogue records: AES-256-GCM on-device encryption
Key = user password + hardware binding (Secure Enclave / Keystore)
The server can never decrypt. Even if the server is compromised, the data is meaningless.

6. How to Call Smartphone Native Features?

LegalShield uses GPS, camera, microphone, SMS, and other smartphone features. Implementation methods are as follows.

6.1 GPS Location

Use: nearest support search, emergency location send, evidence GPS tagging

PlatformAPI / PackageCode Example
Flutter `geolocator` Position pos = await Geolocator.getCurrentPosition();
double lat = pos.latitude;
double lng = pos.longitude;
React Native `@react-native-community/geolocation` Geolocation.getCurrentPosition(pos => {
const {latitude, longitude} = pos.coords;
});
PWA (Web) Geolocation API navigator.geolocation.getCurrentPosition(pos => {
const {latitude, longitude} = pos.coords;
});
⚠️ Privacy Protection Principles
• GPS coordinates are NOT stored on server, used only for on-device facility search
• Emergency SMS sends only prefecture/municipality-level blurred location
• Users can revoke location permission anytime. Other features remain usable after revocation
6.2 Camera (Evidence Photography)

Use: injury photos, accident scenes, property damage, LINE screenshot preservation

PlatformAPI / PackageDescription
Flutter `image_picker` + `camera` Auto-embed timestamp + GPS coordinates + SHA-256 hash watermark after capture
React Native `react-native-image-picker` Auto-attach tamper-proof metadata (EXIF + digital signature)
PWA `<input type="file" accept="image/*">` Browser sandbox limitations. For web MVP. Native APP recommended for production.
6.3 Microphone (Recording & Voice Input)

Use: incident statement recording, call recording (with consent), AI voice dialogue

PlatformAPI / PackageDescription
Flutter `flutter_sound` + `speech_to_text` Real-time transcription during recording, AI auto keyword extraction
React Native `@react-native-voice/voice` Google Speech-to-Text integration, high-accuracy Japanese
🔒 Recording Privacy Design
• Recording files are NOT uploaded to server; on-device Whisper model transcribes, then audio files are deleted
• Only text summaries uploaded (anonymized keywords like "DV, injury, yesterday, husband")
• Call recording requires consent from both parties (consent dialog shown in APP)
6.4 SMS Short Message (Emergency Alert)

Use: auto-send to trusted contacts and support agencies after 10-sec countdown

PlatformAPI / PackageCode Example
Flutter `flutter_sms` sendSMS(message: msg, recipients: ["080-XXXX-XXXX"]);
React Native `react-native-sms` Same as above, calls system SMS to send
📱 Silent Mode
• Emergency SMS sent silently, no sound or vibration
• To ensure the perpetrator does not notice the victim is calling for help
• SMS body template:
"[AUTO-SEND] ○○ (name) has encountered an emergency.
Location: Tokyo ○○ Ward (GPS coordinates not sent)
Time: 2026/05/14 14:32
This message was auto-generated."
